Game: "Flappy bird"

Instructions to play the game: 
	Whenever you want to play the game and you just need to open game page and press on with your mouse on the canvas in the
middle of the page for game to be displayed.
	It is the same when you lost the game and want to try again. For game screen to show up just press the mouse button on
the canvas.
To get the bird moving:
Press spacebar on your keyboard.
